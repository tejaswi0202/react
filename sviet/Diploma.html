Admission Process

  1. Fill out the online application form on our website.

  2. Submit the required documents, including academic transcripts, statement of purpose, and letters of recommendation.

  3. Appear for the entrance examination (if applicable) and/or personal interview.

  4. Wait for the admission results to be announced.
